MakeStack Protocol
===================

**ID** | **name**
:-----:|:-----:
 0x00  | (reserved)
 0x01  | ignored (unsupported) alert
 0x02  | qos
 0x03  | qos_reply
 0x04  | nonce
 0x05  | hmac
 0x0a  | device_id
 0x0b  | device_info
 0x0c  | log
 0x0d  | app_version
 0x0e  | crashdump
 0x10  | store
 0x11  | os_update_request
 0x12  | os_image_url
 0x13  | app_update_request
 0x14  | app_image_url
