Simple Management/Messaging System
===================================

**ID** | **name**
:-----:|:-----:
 0x00  | (reserved)
 0x01  | version
 0x02  | qos
 0x03  | qos_reply
 0x04  | nonce
 0x05  | (reserved)
 0x0a  | device_id
 0x0b  | device_info (0-2 bits: state, 3th bit: debug mode)
 0x0c  | log
 0x0d  | crashdump
 0x10  | os_version
 0x11  | app_version
 0x20  | store
