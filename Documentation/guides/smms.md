---
title: SMMS
---

**ID** | **name**
:-----:|:-----:
 0x00  | (reserved)
 0x01  | version
 0x02  | qos
 0x03  | qos_reply
 0x04  | nonce
 0x06  | hmac
 0x07  | timestamp
 0x0a  | device_id
 0x0b  | device_info (0-2 bits: state, 3: debug mode, 4-5: os)
 0x0c  | log
 0x0d  | crashdump
 0x10  | os_version
 0x11  | app_version
 0x12  | os_image_hmac
 0x13  | app_image_hmac
 0x20  | store
